# One-Day Marathon Training Meal Plan

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete training for a marathon with optimized carbohydrate intake and nutrient timing:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3,200 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (130g), Fat (85g), Carbs (420g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with banana (150g), honey (20g), and whole milk (240g). Whole wheat toast (50g) with almond butter (15g).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Pre-Workout Snack (9:30 AM - 1 hour before run):** White bread (60g) with jam (30g) and a banana (120g).
    * Protein: 6g, Fat: 2g, Carbs: 65g

* **Post-Workout Recovery Drink (11:30 AM - immediately after 90-min run):** Chocolate milk (500g) with a granola bar (50g).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 85g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Grilled chicken breast (150g), jasmine rice (120g cooked), steamed carrots (150g), and olive oil (10g).
    * Protein: 42g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 85g

* **Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM):** Greek yogurt (200g, plain), granola (40g), and berries (100g).
    * Protein: 20g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 60g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM):** Lean ground turkey (160g), whole wheat pasta (110g cooked), marinara sauce (150g), and parmesan cheese (15g).
    * Protein: 40g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 70g

* **Evening Snack (9:30 PM):** Rice cakes (40g) with honey (15g) and a glass of milk (240g).
    * Protein: 8g, Fat: 4g, Carbs: 50g

This meal plan emphasizes high-quality carbohydrates for sustained energy, strategic pre- and post-workout nutrition timing to enhance performance and recovery, and adequate protein distribution throughout the day to support muscle adaptation during marathon training.
